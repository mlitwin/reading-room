Vector fields and flows

Matthew Litwin 2026-05-18

A vector field is a smoothly varying choice of tangent vector at every point. Built on top of vector fields are their integral curves and flows, and the two derivative operators — the Lie bracket of two vector fields, and the Lie derivative along a vector field — that turn vector fields into the basic machinery of dynamical geometry.
