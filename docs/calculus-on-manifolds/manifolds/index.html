Manifolds

Matthew Litwin 2026-05-18

A smooth manifold is a topological space that locally looks like Rn\mathbb{R}^nRn and on which one can do calculus. The three pages here cover the basic objects: the manifold itself, the tangent vectors that live at a point, and the cotangent covectors that pair with them.
