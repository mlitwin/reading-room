Differential forms

Matthew Litwin 2026-06-23

Differential forms are the algebraic objects you integrate over manifolds. The three pieces of the algebra — the wedge product, the exterior derivative, and pullback — combine to give Stokes’s theorem its full generality and to give de Rham cohomology its definition.
