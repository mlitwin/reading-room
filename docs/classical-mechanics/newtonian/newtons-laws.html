Configuration space

The configuration space QQQ of a mechanical system is the manifold of all possible positions of the system, with constraints already enforced. For NNN unconstrained particles in R3\mathbb{R}^3R3, Q=R3NQ = \mathbb{R}^{3N}Q=R3N. For a pendulum, Q=S1Q = S^1Q=S1.
