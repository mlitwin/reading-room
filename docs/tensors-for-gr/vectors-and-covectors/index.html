Vectors and covectors

Matthew Litwin 2026-05-21

Tensors are built out of vectors and covectors. Before the multilinear-algebra machinery starts, this section gets the two basic objects on the table — what they are, how their components transform, how they pair — in both the embedded and abstract pictures, and in both the index and coordinate-free notations.

Three pages: the tangent vector at a point; the dual cotangent vector at a point; the same objects worked out on S2S^2S2 in two coordinate charts.
