On the sphere

Transformation diagnostic

Both objects above transform correctly: changing chart, recomputing the components from the rule, and checking that the result is the same as starting fresh in the new chart, is a finite (and tedious) check. The defining property of a tensor — the multiplicative transformation law of the previous page — is what licenses calling these objects intrinsic features of the sphere rather than chart artifacts.
