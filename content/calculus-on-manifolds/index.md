This isn't a textbook. It's a one-stop reference for the calculus-on-manifolds curriculum that someone who has seen the material before can use to recover any of the standard definitions, formulas, and theorems without paging through a thicker book.

Notation throughout: $M, N$ are smooth manifolds; $p \in M$; $T_p M$ is the tangent space at $p$ with basis $\partial_i := \partial/\partial x^i$ in local coordinates $x^i = \varphi^i$; $T^*_p M$ is the cotangent space with dual basis $dx^i$; $\Omega^k(M)$ is the space of smooth $k$-forms; $X, Y, Z$ are vector fields; $\omega, \eta$ are differential forms; Einstein summation is in force throughout.
