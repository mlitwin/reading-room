---
title: Red
---

Red is the loudest of the three. It demands more attention than it should — in a painting it pulls the eye whether or not you intended. The hardest part of working with red is restraint: using just enough that the rest of the canvas can still speak.

Compare with [blue](blue.md), which retreats instead of advances, or [yellow](yellow.md), which sits somewhere in the middle without forcing the issue.
