---
title: Yellow
---

Yellow is direct. It's the color a child reaches for when drawing the sun, and that isn't an accident — yellow points at things. It doesn't have red's demands or blue's depth. The risk with yellow is that there's nowhere to hide: it shows every wobble in the brushwork.

Compare with [red](red.md) and [blue](blue.md), or return [to the index](index.md).
