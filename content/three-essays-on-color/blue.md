---
title: Blue
---

Blue recedes. Atmospheric perspective is mostly blue's doing — distant mountains, far water, the haze on the horizon. A scene painted without blue feels close, even claustrophobic, no matter how spatially deep the artist meant it to be.

Compare with [red](red.md), which advances, or [yellow](yellow.md), which neither retreats nor presses.
